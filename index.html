About Us

At Trinity Motor Works, performance is our purpose.

We specialize in the design and engineering of proprietary aftermarket performance parts for automotive enthusiasts who demand more from their machines. By combining modern precision manufacturing with timeless mechanical principles, we deliver components that push boundaries — in performance, durability, and innovation.

Whether we're developing in-house solutions or distributing carefully selected third-party products, every part we offer is driven by one goal: to modernize tradition without compromise.

Our Expertise

At Trinity Motor Works, our core focus is high-performance components for classic BMWs, Subarus, and universal platforms. From suspension and driveline upgrades to precision hardware and fabricated solutions, every part is engineered for enthusiasts who demand reliable performance with a custom edge.

Our automotive work is backed by deep roots in aerospace, military, and medical engineering — industries where quality, safety, and precision aren't optional. We bring this mindset to every part we design, combining hands-on experience with advanced engineering principles to deliver results that go far beyond bolt-on upgrades.
